Who I am

My name is Will Van de Walle. I am an aspiring professional artist and lover of creativity. I like to build, draw, write, and challenge my brain. I am here to write about that and that process for the good of any other aspiring artists.

What I like

I like to hang out with my friends, and draw, write, all things that I've mentioned before, of course.

What I hate

I hate: lettuce, being bored, not being able to do ANYTHING but sit there, and overall not being creative or productive.
